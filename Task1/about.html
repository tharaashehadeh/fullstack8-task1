Information about One Thousand and One Nights Restaurant A Middle Eastern restaurant with a classic and elegant decor and a large area, which makes its atmosphere comfortable and suitable for families and groups. When you visit the restaurant, you can enjoy many delicious Middle Eastern foods, including appetizers such as hummus, muttabal, makdous, and pickles, in addition to salads, including tabbouleh, fattoush, and watercress salad, and appetizers, including kibbeh, falafel, and samosas. As for the main dishes, various grill dishes and casseroles are available, such as salmon and mansaf casserole, meat sajiya, pizza, pasta, and burgers, in addition to special Friday dishes, including stuffed neck, grilled lamb, grape leaves, and many other delicious dishes.
